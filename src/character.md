This page will explain character creation.

Summary:
* Assign stats: +2, +1, +1, +0, +0, -1

* Pick a Background
* For each level, pick a talent
* For each level, pick a ribbon

Stats:
* Attributes:
  * Agility
  * Strength
  * Finesse
  * Instinct
  * Presence
  * Knowledge
* Defenses:
  * Armor
  * Spirit
* Health
* Speed
